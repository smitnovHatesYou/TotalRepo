About ASL

About ASL

ASL is a complete visual language with its own grammar and rules. It is expressed through hand shapes, facial expressions, and body movements.

ASL is not a form of pantomime. It has its own syntax and structure, just like spoken languages.

ASL is used by over half a million people in the United States and Canada. It is the 4th most commonly used language in the US.
